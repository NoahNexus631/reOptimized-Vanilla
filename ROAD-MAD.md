# Roadmap

This roadmap outlines the current development plans for **re:Optimized Vanilla**.

> The roadmap is subject to change as development progresses.

---

# Vision

re:Optimized Vanilla aims to provide a **vanilla-friendly Minecraft experience** by improving:

- Performance
- Visuals
- User Interface
- Animations
- Audio
- Quality of Life

without changing vanilla gameplay, progression, world generation, mobs, or game balance.

# Philosophy

Every update follows the same principles:

- Preserve vanilla gameplay.
- Preserve vanilla progression.
- Preserve vanilla world generation.
- Stay client-side only.
- Prioritize stability over feature count.
- Only include carefully selected, high-quality mods.
