# re:Optimized Vanilla v2.0.0-0.1 Beta

**Minecraft:** 26.1.2
**Loader:** NeoForge

> ⚠️ This is the first public **Beta** release for NeoForge.
>
> The NeoForge edition is currently under active development. While the core philosophy of re:Optimized Vanilla remains unchanged, some features from the Fabric edition are temporarily unavailable due to loader compatibility.

---

## Notes

The NeoForge edition intentionally **does not use Forgified Fabric or compatibility layers**.

Only native NeoForge mods are included to ensure:

- Better long-term stability
- Easier maintenance
- Improved compatibility
- Cleaner dependency management
- Better future support
